ABOUT US

Who We Are

Royal Forge Jewellery, renowned as the "Monarch of the Gold World," emanates from the vibrant realm of Sri Lanka, celebrated as the Gem Island. With a kaleidoscope of resplendent gemstones indigenous to Sri Lanka, the brand epitomizes daring ingenuity. Rooted in heritage since its inception in 1950, Royal Forge Jewellery was first established by Mr.Perera and subsequently entrusted to his descendants Mr.Fernando and Ms.Athapaththu. It has curated a myriad of captivating jewellery pieces using exquisite Sri Lankan gemstones, meeting the evolving tastes of successive generations of connoisseurs.

With over four decades of illustrious existence, Royal Forge Jewellery stands tall as a paragon of excellence in the gold jewellery domain of Sri Lanka. Throughout its illustrious odyssey of innovation, the brand has meticulously crafted masterpieces that have garnered acclaim both locally and globally, nurturing a devoted and expanding clientele. Royal Forge Jewellery's extensive repertoire encompasses jewellery for men, women, and children, each piece bearing the hallmark of timeless elegance.

Embodying a rich heritage and a legacy of trust meticulously cultivated over the years, Royal Forge Jewellery offers bespoke services, catering to discerning customers seeking exquisite adornments for their special occasions or to commemorate significant milestones.

With a robust presence across key cities nationwide, Royal Forge Jewellery prides itself on using ethically sourced and sustainably harvested gemstones, zircons, and refined gold to fashion enduring symbols of personal adornment. The brand's enduring bonds with patrons are forged through a commitment to excellence, impeccable service, and the creation of bespoke, handcrafted jewellery pieces.

At the heart of Royal Forge Jewellery lies the craftsmanship of its artisans, the vision of its designers, and an unwavering dedication to using only the finest materials, all underpinned by a profound devotion to its cherished clientele, thus becoming an integral part of their cherished memories.



Organizational Vision & Mission

Vision

To be recognized globally as the pinnacle of elegance and innovation in the realm of fine jewellery, where every creation embodies the essence of timeless beauty and cultural heritage.


Mission

At Royal Forge Jewellery, our mission is to passionately craft exquisite jewellery pieces that celebrate the rich tapestry of Sri Lanka's gemstone heritage. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of craftsmanship, ethics, and sustainability, while providing unparalleled service and creating cherished connections with our discerning clientele, ensuring that every piece becomes a treasured symbol of enduring elegance and personal significance
